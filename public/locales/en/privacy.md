# Privacy Policy of Air China Virtual


# 1. Purpose of This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Air China Virtual collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in accordance with the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** (the GDPR text is available at https://gdpr-info.eu/).

Air China Virtual is a non-profit virtual airline organization operating on the VATSIM network.
We only collect the data necessary for member management and operational safety.

------

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All members of Air China Virtual
- All former members (whose data is still stored within the permitted retention period)
- All applicants and prospective members
- Anyone who interacts with Air China Virtual systems (website, Discord, operations systems, etc.)

------

# 3. Legal Basis for Processing

Air China Virtual processes personal data on the following legal bases:

- **Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))**
  Consent granted when you apply to join or voluntarily submit information.
- **Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))**
  For identity verification, maintaining operational safety, supporting organizational operations, and complying with VATSIM policies.

Members may withdraw consent at any time.

------

# 4. Data We Collect

Air China Virtual only collects the minimum data required for identity verification, network connectivity, and participation in operations.

### 4.1 Personal Data

- Name (or, upon request, “First name + last initial”)
- Email address
- VATSIM CID
- Discord username / Discord user ID
- QQ number

### 4.2 Data We Do Not Collect

To minimize risk, Air China Virtual **does not** collect the following:

- Phone numbers
- Government ID numbers
- Residential addresses
- Facial or biometric information
- Payment or financial information
- Any special categories of sensitive data as defined in GDPR Article 9

Air China Virtual does not carry out profiling or automated decision-making.

------

# 5. How We Collect Data

Data is primarily collected through:

- Membership application or registration forms
- Information submitted by members themselves
- Publicly accessible VATSIM API data
- Discord server member information

Visitors can browse public content anonymously.

------

# 6. Purposes of Processing

### 6.1 Membership Management

- Identity verification
- Roster maintenance
- Communication with members
- Operations system maintenance

### 6.2 Operational Needs

- Participation in VATSIM events and activities
- Event planning and coordination

### 6.3 Communications

- Event announcements
- Safety notices
- Administrative updates

### 6.4 Cookies (Website)

If Cookies are used, they are only used for:

- Maintaining login sessions
- Saving user preferences

No advertising or tracking Cookies are used.

------

# 7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Air China Virtual **does not sell, trade, or commercially share** personal data.

Data is only shared within the following scope:

### 7.1 Sharing with VATSIM

Only provided when required for:

- Compliance verification
- Security reviews
- Organizational audit requirements

### 7.2 Service Providers

Limited to platforms used for organizational operations, such as:

- VATSIM
- VAMSYS
- Website hosting services
- QQ
- Discord

These services only process data on instruction and provide protection equivalent to the GDPR.

### 7.3 Public Information Requirements

To comply with VATSIM policies, Air China Virtual may publicly disclose the following information:

- Name
- VATSIM CID
- Pilot status (active / inactive)

Email addresses and internal records are **never directly disclosed**.

------

# 8. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Cloud services used by Air China Virtual may store data outside the EU/EEA and comply with the requirements of GDPR Chapter V.

By submitting data, members consent to international transfers under the following conditions:

- Appropriate safeguards are in place
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) are used when necessary
- Service providers offer protection equivalent to the GDPR

Air China Virtual will not share data with any government or commercial entity.

------

# 9. Data Retention

Air China Virtual retains personal data only for as long as necessary:

- Active members: retained for the duration of membership
- Former members: retained for up to **12 months** to maintain organizational integrity
- Operational data: according to VAMSYS platform policies

After the retention period ends, data will be securely deleted.

Members may request early deletion at any time.

------

# 10. Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR Chapter III, you have the right to:

- **Be informed**
- **Access your personal data**
- **Rectify your data**
- **Erase your data (“right to be forgotten”)**
- **Restrict processing**
- **Withdraw consent**
- **Data portability**

A request to delete personal data will result in termination of Air China Virtual membership.

To exercise these rights, please contact the data controller.
